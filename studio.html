About

Shutter Space Studio®

Multifunctional Photo and Video Studio for Rent



Driven entirely by the idea of offering our clients the most modern and innovative services in the field of photography and videography, we have created a modern filming studio in Ruse.

The studio features 5 different sets that can be altered in various ways. To eliminate any external factors and ensure complete control over the scenes and the final production outcome, we have light- and soundproofed the space itself. The studio is ready to meet the growing needs of our clients and the challenges of the new world.
