Video and Photo Studio for Rent in Ruse

Shutter Space Studio® is a modern rental photography and video studio located in the heart of Ruse. The studio is available for various purposes, including podcast recording, product reviews, as well as different types of video productions and photo sessions. Here, you can not only rent the necessary equipment for your projects but also receive professional guidance, advice, and assistance.
